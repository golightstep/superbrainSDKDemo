Distributed System Architecture

This setup spans 3 GCP regions. SuperBrain acts as the lightning-fast data highway, fanning out market data to all nodes simultaneously via a peer-to-peer fabric. Redis acts as the central traffic controller, ensuring global order limits and persistent queuing are respected across the entire cluster.

When a node crashes, the Raft algorithm ensures a new leader is elected to maintain the control plane (Redis & Coordinator), while the SuperBrain data fabric continues to deliver sub-millisecond price updates to surviving nodes.
